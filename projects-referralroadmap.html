Referral Roadmap

Designing an AI-supported system for future mental healthcare referrals

Mental healthcare System design AI integration Future scenario

Overview

This project explores how AI can support the referral process between general practitioners (GP) and GGZ in Dutch mental healthcare. The outcome is a horizon-based roadmap aligning stakeholder needs with ethical and regulatory constraints.

Research & Insights

Interviews, co-creation, and system mapping surfaced two core barriers and three opportunity areas.

Concept & Design

AI-supported triage, information handover, and feedback loops across GP–GGZ touchpoints.

Outcome & Reflection

The roadmap informed the thesis and stakeholder workshops; it clarified systemic shifts and risks.
